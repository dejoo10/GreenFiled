Meet Our Founders

The people behind our vision and success

Temitope Olunukunde

Tope Olabokunde brings a wealth of agricultural expertise and experience to GreenField Capital. With over 7 years of hands-on involvement in managing various agro-farm startups, Tope possesses a deep understanding of the intricacies of agricultural operations. His passion for the industry and commitment to sustainable farming practices have been instrumental in driving the success of numerous agricultural ventures. Tope holds a Bachelor's degree in Agriculture from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife, where he gained a strong foundation in agricultural sciences. His comprehensive knowledge of crop cultivation, livestock management, and farm operations ensures that GreenField Capital’s farming activities are executed with the utmost professionalism and efficiency.

Adedeji Babalola

Adedeji Babalola is a driven entrepreneur and environmental specialist with a master's degree in Circular Economy. Alongside his academic accomplishments, Adedeji possesses a minor degree in Entrepreneurial Studies and brings a diverse skill set to the team. With a background in business development, customer service, and sales, Adedeji contributes his business acumen and strategic thinking to the growth and development of GreenField Capital. His deep understanding of sustainable practices, coupled with his expertise in environmental stewardship, helps guide the company in integrating circular economy principles throughout its operations. Adedeji's entrepreneurial mindset and commitment to delivering exceptional service contribute to the overall success of the company.

Busayo Ojo

Busayo Elijah Ojo is a seasoned professional in Agricultural Economics and Extension with 17 years of experience in the field. Hailing from Owo, Ondo State, Busayo graduated from the Federal University of Technology, Minna. His extensive background encompasses sustainable agricultural practices and farm management, making him an invaluable asset to any agricultural project. With a passion for visiting nature, playing games, watching movies and reading, Busayo balances his professional life with enriching personal interests. He holds the prestigious badge of a certified global Gap Farm Assurer, underscoring his commitment to maintaining high standards in agricultural production, management and sustainability. Busayo’s experience and dedication to excellence ensure the successful implementation of sustainable and innovative agricultural solutions for the company.
